# King County House Sales Interactive Dashboard

This project explores residential real estate sales data from King County, 
Washington, which includes Seattle and its surrounding areas. The goal was 
to make sense of what actually drives home prices in one of the most 
competitive housing markets in the US and present those findings in a way 
that anyone can interact with and understand.

The dashboard lets you filter sales by date, bedroom count, square footage, 
and condition of the home, so you can slice the data however makes sense 
for what you are trying to learn. Whether you want to see how waterfront 
properties compare to inland ones or how prices shifted across different 
neighborhoods, the filters make that exploration straightforward.

Some of the key things the dashboard surfaces are price trends over time, 
a geographic map of where sales are concentrated, and distributions of 
home condition and size. Together these views give a pretty complete 
picture of the market without overwhelming you with numbers.

**Tools used:** Tableau  
**Dataset:** King County House Sales (publicly available dataset covering 
2014 to 2015 sales records)
